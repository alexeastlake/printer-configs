## Notes

- **Best for:** functional/structural parts, things needing heat resistance, weather resistance, or some flex.
- **Bed adhesion warning:** PETG sticks *very* well to smooth PEI. **Always let the bed cool fully before removing the print** - pulling while warm can take chunks out of the PEI coating.
