## Temps

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Nozzle | **200°C** |
| Bed | **55°C** |

Temp tower run: 190-230°C, 5°C steps. 200°C gave the best balance of bridging and minimal stringing. 190-195°C showed thin stringing and risked weak layer adhesion, 210°C and above showed progressive stringing and bridge sag.
